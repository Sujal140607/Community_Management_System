# 🌉 Samaj Setu  
### Bridge for Community Management System

Samaj Setu is a web-based Community Management System designed to digitally manage and streamline the administrative operations of a Samaj (community organization).  

It centralizes member records, community classifications, financial contributions, and reporting into one secure and scalable platform.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Traditional community record-keeping is manual and fragmented, leading to:
- Data duplication
- Record mismanagement
- Manual calculation errors
- Lack of transparency
- Slow reporting processes

Samaj Setu solves these issues with a structured digital platform.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Digitize community records
- Centralize administrative data
- Ensure data accuracy and validation
- Automate reports and calculations
- Maintain financial transparency
- Provide secure role-based access

---
